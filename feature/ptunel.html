How to Setup Powertunnel

  1. First, download Powertunnel APP from Press here.
  2. Then, click on the "Powertunnel" button.
  3. It will copy the Powertunnel host Coppy -> https://gtpshost.com/Vintageps.txt
  4. Open the Powertunnel app, click the three-line icon, and click on the host settings icon.
  5. Paste the copied Powertunnel host URL. Also, change the host update period from 12 hours to "on start."
  6. Now you can log in to your GTPS!
