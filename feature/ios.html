How to Setup Surge 5 for iOS

  1. Download the app called Surge 5 from the App Store.
  2. Press "OK".
  3. Click on "Default.conf".
  4. Click on the "IMPORT" part, then select Download Profile from URL.
  5. Paste the URL you copied by clicking the iOS host button above, then click "OK" and "Done".
  6. Press "SETUP", then agree to the policy by clicking "OK" and "Allow" for VPN Configuration.
  7. You're done! Open Growtopia and connect!
